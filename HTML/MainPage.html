Game Explanation

Basic play:

Click on the floating bubbles to pop them and rack up points! Weapon charges are added by the amount of points you have.

Weapons are used by clicking on the weapon button and then clicking again in the play area, you can cancel the weapon pickup by clicking the same weapon button again.

Weapon Explanation:

  • Bomb - pops all bubbles in its circle when used.
  • Freeze Ray - stops the movement of all bubbles in its circle when used.
  • Bouncy Ball - creates a ball that bounces around the play area for 10 seconds that pops all the bubbles it touches.
